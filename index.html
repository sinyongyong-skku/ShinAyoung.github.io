About Me

Nice to meet you

Hi, I'm Shin Ayoung, a Dance major at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU). While studying dance, I discovered a fascinating connection between the language of movement and the patterns found in data.

Through the Arts and Big Data course, I've been learning to visualize data using tools like Python, Streamlit, and Plotly — applying my artistic eye to build interactive dashboards that make complex information feel intuitive and beautiful.

I dream of a world where data art merges food culture, nutritional science, and the aesthetics of movement into one cohesive experience.

Projects

Final Project

01
Nourish
🌿 Ingredient & Recipe Data Hub Dashboard

An interactive dashboard that visualizes the nutritional data of whole-food ingredients as radar charts, revealing each ingredient's unique "Nutritional Persona" at a glance. Integrated with the Spoonacular API for real-time recipe search and a favorites manager to save recipes you love. Rather than chasing trending food content, Nourish puts objective nutritional data at the center of ingredient exploration.
